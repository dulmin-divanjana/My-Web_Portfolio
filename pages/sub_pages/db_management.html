Data Base Management



Manage databases by designing, implementing, and maintaining
database systems to ensure data integrity, security, and accessibility.
They develop database schemas, optimize queries, and troubleshoot
performance issues. Additionally, they handle data migration, backup,
and recovery processes, ensuring continuous availability and
reliability of data for applications and users.
